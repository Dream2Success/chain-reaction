How to play
Tap any cell to spark it. If a cell gets more orbs than its neighbor-capacity, it explodes and sends one orb to each neighbour (up/down/left/right) and converts them to the exploding color — this can cause chain reactions!

Goal: clear all orbs, or create the largest chain. Try different sizes and fill densities.
